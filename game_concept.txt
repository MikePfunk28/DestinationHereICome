# Destination Here I Come - Game Concept

## 1. Main Goal:
The "Destination" is Aethelgard.
Player wants to find Aethelgard for power/knowledge.

## 2. Initial Setting:
Game starts in The Shambles, a poor district in Veridia.
Veridia has Merchant Guilds, City Watch, Underground Networks, Ancient Cults.

## 3. Player's Initial Role:
Player is an outsider in Veridia.
Goal: Find Aethelgard.
Starts with nothing. Survival, info gathering, resource building.

== Introduction Story ==
Veridia is a city of contrasts. You start in The Shambles, an outsider aiming for Aethelgard. Survival is key.

== First Chapter Goal/Conclusion ==
Goal: Complete package quest from Wary Informant, upgrade hideout, defeat Scrawny Thug.
Conclusion: Informant points to a scholar in Scribe's Quarter who knows of Aethelgard.
